2. Customer Onboarding
======================

Perform following steps to onboard a new customer.

1.Click on Trace9 Admin from the left-side menu, and select Customers from the options 


.. image:: /image/13.png

2.A window with a list of customers will appear. 


.. image:: /image/14.png

3.To add a new customer, click on “Add” button on the top-left side of the customer window. 


.. image:: /image/15.png

4.A panel for adding a new customer will appear on the right. 


.. image:: /image/16.png

5.Provide the required information and click on the add button.


.. image:: /image/17.png

6.New customer will be added


.. image:: /image/18.png
